self-hosted, free, and easy to use

Solo is a free task management app for freelancers. Create track time, manage tasks, upload files, and keep notes all in the same place. And everything is stays on your server so your data is always private.

    Beautiful Design

    The simple, elegant, and intuitive interface means Solo is much easier to use than other free project management apps. It works great on mobile too.

    Simple Setup

    Easy to use installation wizard to guide you through the process. Requires a linux server, PHP 5.3+, and MySQL.

    Free Forever

    Solo is free forever and there are no hidden fees. If you like it, feel free to share with your friends or on your social networks
